My Work

Curtains

Custom window treatments are the mark of a well-designed space, but no two windows have the same design needs. I can sew draperies, all styles of fabric shades, valances, cornices, and swags made to your exact specifications.

Slipcovers

If a couch or chair is beginning to look too worn, a slipcover is an easy, inexpensive way to update the look of your furniture. They’re easily removed, washable, and can be changed quickly to accommodate changes in decor. Your favorite old chair will look brand new.

Other

If it’s made from fabric, I can sew it. Bedding, decorative pillows, indoor and outdoor cushions, nursery items, table coverings, and pet beds are some of my clients’ most requested items. Decide what you want, and let’s get to work!

About Me

I absolutely love to sew. I learned how when I was eight, while visiting my favorite aunt and uncle in Oregon. My aunt helped me sew a simple dress and I was hooked! I went on to sew prom dresses in high school and then, when I had my own home, made my own bedding and window treatments.

My first career was working as an emergency room nurse, which I did for 20 years. I loved that too, but the hours were strenuous and didn’t leave much time for me to spend with my four boys, who were very young at the time. I decided that I wanted to work from home so I could be around them more often, so I left my job and started my own sewing business! That was 15 years ago, and I feel so lucky to have been able to be there for my family and still do what I love.
